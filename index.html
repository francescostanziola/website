Research

My research focuses on the digitization of architectural cultural heritage, with particular attention to the phenomenological aspects that characterize these extraordinary testimonies of humanity's past. I primarily work with laser scanning, photogrammetry, and I am exploring methods of neural rendering. My other academic interests revolve around the graphic languages of representation and communication, architectural surveying, and design.
